Elshan Ismailzada or better known by his stage name H A N S A A is a music producer and Dj from Azerbaijan. Born in 1999 in Azerbaijan, Sumgait.
H A N S A A 's childhood passion to music motivated him to take his first production classes when he was 15.
Alongside with his studies he joined several production courses as a DJ producer before he moved to Turkey for university in 2016.
H A N S A A 's style combines Deep House, Progressive House, Bass House and many other categories with deep and emotional vocals.His first official track called ''I'm Coming Home'' was released by Baijan Records in 2020.
